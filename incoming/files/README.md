<div class="aa-divider"><span>How to Get Started</span></div>

## 🗺️ Recommended Learning Path

<div class="aa-timeline">

<div class="aa-timeline-item">
  <h4>Step 1 — Choose Your Target Exam</h4>
  <p>Go to JEE, NEET, or School section. Identify which chapters you are currently studying.</p>
</div>

<div class="aa-timeline-item">
  <h4>Step 2 — Set Up Your Study System</h4>
  <p>Read the Study Systems section and choose your revision cycle (1D → 7D → 30D → 90D).</p>
</div>

<div class="aa-timeline-item">
  <h4>Step 3 — Use the Chapter Template</h4>
  <p>Every chapter follows the AI Atlas Universal Template — theory, AI prompts, memory techniques, PYQ analysis, and checklist.</p>
</div>

<div class="aa-timeline-item">
  <h4>Step 4 — Add AI to Your Workflow</h4>
  <p>Browse the Prompt Library and pick prompts for your current chapter. Use with ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.</p>
</div>

<div class="aa-timeline-item">
  <h4>Step 5 — Track & Revise</h4>
  <p>Log your mistakes, complete revision cycles, and aim for the Chapter Mastery checklist before moving on.</p>
</div>

</div>
