Botany Prompts

BIO-01 — Plant Kingdom Classification

"Help me create a classification key for Plant Kingdom
 (Thallophyta → Angiosperms). For each division:
 distinguishing features, examples, NEET question types."

BIO-02 — Photosynthesis Process

"Explain photosynthesis completely in two parts:
 1. Light reactions: Z-scheme, photosystems, ATP/NADPH production
 2. Calvin Cycle: carbon fixation, all enzymes, outputs
 Include the exact numbers NEET tests (wavelengths, molecules)."

Zoology Prompts

BIO-03 — Human Circulation

"Trace the path of a red blood cell through the entire
 human circulatory system. Include:
 every heart chamber, every valve, pulmonary vs systemic,
 oxygenation events. Name every structure NCERT names."

BIO-04 — Genetics Problems

"Solve this genetics problem: [problem].
 Show: Punnett square, gamete types, phenotypic ratio,
 genotypic ratio. Explain the law being tested."

BIO-05 — Molecular Genetics

"Explain DNA replication step by step.
 Name every enzyme, its function, the direction of synthesis.
 Include Okazaki fragments and why they exist.
 End with 5 NEET MCQs from this topic."
