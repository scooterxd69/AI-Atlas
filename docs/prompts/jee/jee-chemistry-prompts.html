Physical Chemistry Prompts

P-CHEM-01 — Electrochemistry

"Explain electrochemical cells completely.
 Galvanic vs Electrolytic. EMF calculation using Nernst equation.
 Relationship between ΔG, E°cell, and K.
 Include 3 JEE-level numericals with solutions."

P-CHEM-02 — Equilibrium

"I'm solving equilibrium problem: [problem].
 Step 1: Identify if Kp or Kc should be used.
 Step 2: Write equilibrium expression.
 Step 3: Use ICE table.
 Step 4: Solve and verify the answer makes physical sense."

Organic Chemistry Prompts

P-CHEM-03 — Reaction Mechanism

"Explain the mechanism of [reaction name] completely.
 Show: electron movement with arrows, intermediate structures,
 why the major product forms (not just which product),

P-CHEM-04 — Reaction Map

"Create a reaction map starting from [compound].
 Show all reagents and products in a flowchart format.
 Include only JEE-relevant reactions.
 Mark which reactions have been asked in JEE."
