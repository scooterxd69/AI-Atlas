How to Use YouTube With AI

Don't just watch — use this workflow:

  1. Watch the concept video
  2. Then open Claude and paste:
"I just watched a video on [TOPIC] for JEE.
 I understand the basics now. Give me:
 1. The 3 most common JEE question types on this topic
 2. A formula sheet for this chapter
 3. Two solved examples at JEE Advanced difficulty"

→ This combines passive video learning with active AI-assisted problem solving.
