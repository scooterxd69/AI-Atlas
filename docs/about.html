About AI Atlas

A free AI learning atlas for serious students.

AI Atlas helps JEE, NEET and school students use AI as a study system: prompts, workflows, tools, resources and exam-focused guides in one open-source platform.

Explore Systems Search Atlas

Mission

AI Atlas is built to make high-quality AI-assisted learning available to every student, without paid coaching dependency or complicated tools.

What It Includes

  • Study systems for JEE, NEET and school students.
  • Prompt libraries for concept clarity, revision, mock analysis and exam preparation.
  • Workflow guides that turn AI tools into repeatable study habits.
  • Curated tools, resources and career guidance.

Open Source

The project is free to use, adapt and improve. Students and contributors can help expand the atlas through the GitHub repository.
