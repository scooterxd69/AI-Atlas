Gemini for Students

AI Atlas → AI Tools → Gemini for Students

Gemini is Google's AI. Best for Hindi-medium students and Google Docs/Drive integration.

Why Gemini?

  • Hindi support — Best Hindi responses of all free AI tools
  • Google integration — Works with Google Docs, Sheets, Drive
  • 1 million token context — Upload entire textbooks
  • Free — Gemini 1.5 Flash is completely free

Setup

  1. Go to gemini.google.com
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Start chatting — no additional setup needed

For Google Workspace integration: Gemini → Extensions → Enable Google Workspace

Hindi Study Prompts

"मुझे [अध्याय] के बारे में समझाएं। 
 मैं JEE की तैयारी कर रहा हूं।
 आसान भाषा में, उदाहरणों के साथ।"
"[सूत्र] का derivation step-by-step बताएं।
 हर step का कारण भी बताएं।"

Upload NCERT and Ask Questions

  1. Click the + icon in Gemini
  2. Upload your NCERT PDF or photo
  3. Ask in Hindi or English
"[Uploaded: NCERT Biology Chapter 10]
 इस chapter के सबसे important points क्या हैं NEET के लिए?
 Diagram के साथ explain करें।"
