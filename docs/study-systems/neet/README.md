## NEET Strategy at a Glance

> **Biology = 360 marks (90 questions) · Physics + Chemistry = 360 marks · Total = 720 marks**

The AI Atlas NEET philosophy:
1. **NCERT is the syllabus** — every word before any reference book
2. **AI for understanding, not skipping** — use prompts to go deeper into NCERT
3. **Diagram mastery** — AI generates labelled diagrams for practice
4. **PYQ pattern analysis** — AI identifies patterns per chapter per year
5. **Assertion-Reason practice** — AI generates unlimited A-R questions
