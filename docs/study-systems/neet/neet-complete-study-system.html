Rule #1: Biology gets 60% of your time. Always.

NCERT-First Rule

Every single word of NCERT Biology Class 11 and 12 must be memorised before you open any reference book.

NEET Biology questions are almost entirely NCERT-based. Students who read H.C. Verma for Biology are wasting time.

AI's role in NCERT mastery:

"I'm reading NCERT Biology Class 11 Chapter [X — Chapter Name].
 After I finish reading, I'll ask you questions. Right now, create
 a list of 20 things from this chapter that NEET has previously
 tested. I'll use it as my reading guide."

2-Year Plan (Class 11 + 12)

Class 11 Year

Term 1 (June–September):

  • Biology: Cell Biology (Chapters 8–10) + Biomolecules
  • Physics: Laws of Motion + Work Energy + Thermodynamics
  • Chemistry: Mole Concept + Atomic Structure + Chemical Bonding

Term 2 (October–January):

  • Biology: Plant Kingdom + Morphology + Anatomy + Ecology
  • Physics: Gravitation + Properties of Matter
  • Chemistry: Equilibrium + Redox + Hydrogen + s-Block

Term 3 (February–May):

  • Biology: Plant Physiology (Photosynthesis + Respiration + Growth)
  • Physics: Oscillations + Waves + Kinetic Theory
  • Chemistry: Organic Basics + Hydrocarbons + Environmental

Class 12 Year

Term 1 (June–September):

  • Biology: Reproduction (Sexual + Asexual) + Genetics (Mendelian)
  • Physics: Electrostatics + Current Electricity
  • Chemistry: Electrochemistry + Chemical Kinetics

Term 2 (October–January):

  • Biology: Molecular Genetics + Evolution + Human Health
  • Physics: Magnetic Effects + EMI + Optics
  • Chemistry: d-Block + Coordination + Biomolecules + Polymers

Term 3 (February–April — Revision Only):

  • Full NCERT revision (Biology 3x, Physics 2x, Chemistry 2x)
  • Daily mock tests
  • Error log review

Diagram Mastery System

NEET tests diagrams heavily. Build your diagram library:

"I'm studying [CHAPTER] for NEET. List all diagrams that
 have been asked in NEET PYQs from this chapter.
 For each diagram: what it shows, what labels are typically
 asked, what questions are asked about it."

Then draw each diagram from memory. Redraw until perfect.
