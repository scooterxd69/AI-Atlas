NEET Physics & Chemistry — Study System

AI Atlas → Study Systems → NEET → Physics + Chemistry

The Supporting Subjects

Physics and Chemistry together are 360 marks — equal to Biology. But most NEET toppers spend only 40% of their time on them. The key is efficiency.

NEET Physics: What's Different From JEE

NEET Physics is NCERT-level. You don't need JEE-level depth.

Focus on:

  • Formula application (not derivation)
  • Simple 1-2 step numericals
  • Conceptual MCQs from NCERT

AI Prompt for NEET Physics:

"Explain [TOPIC] for NEET level (not JEE).
 Give me:
 1. Key formulas with units
 2. 5 NEET-pattern MCQs (concept-based, not difficult numericals)
 3. Common mistakes NEET students make in this topic"

NEET Chemistry: NCERT + Inorganic

NEET Chemistry is more inorganic-heavy than JEE. Physical Chemistry numericals are simpler.

Priority:

  1. NCERT reactions and equations (especially organic + inorganic)
  2. Named reactions in organic
  3. p-Block properties (colours, formulas, reactions)
  4. Biomolecules (from both Chemistry and Biology NCERT)

AI Prompt for Inorganic NEET:

"Summarise [element group] from NCERT for NEET.
 Focus on: physical properties, chemical reactions,
 important compounds, previous NEET questions from this group."
