# AI Atlas Blueprint

## Mission

Build the world's best free AI learning ecosystem for students.

## Target Audience

- IIT JEE Aspirants
- NEET Aspirants
- School Students
- AI Beginners

## Core Components

### AI Tools Database
Curated collection of AI tools organized by category.

### Prompt Library
Ready-to-use prompts for studying, revision, problem solving, research and productivity.

### Study Systems
Complete AI-powered study workflows.

### Educational Workflows
Step-by-step systems for learning and exam preparation.

### Chapter Guides
AI-enhanced chapter learning frameworks.

## Content Principles

- Free First
- Beginner Friendly
- Practical
- Actionable
- Continuously Updated

## V1 Deliverables

- 5 Study Systems
- 50 JEE Prompts
- Universal Chapter Template
- 100 AI Tools Database
- 25 Educational Workflows

## Long-Term Goal

Become the Wikipedia of AI Learning.
