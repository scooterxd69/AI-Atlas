# AI Atlas Vision

AI Atlas is a free AI learning ecosystem.

Goal:
Help students learn faster and use AI effectively without expensive courses.

Target Users:
- JEE Aspirants
- NEET Aspirants
- School Students
- Beginners

Core Pillars:
- AI Tools
- Prompt Library
- Study Systems
- Workflows
- Chapter Guides

Long-Term Goal:
Become the Wikipedia of AI Learning.
